Title: Sannur Valley Cave
Description: Just a 2 hours drive from the city of Cairo, the Sannur Valley Cave is known popularly for its fascinating natural geographical formations of stalagmites and stalactites. There are also a huge quantity of quarries, most of which date back to the ancient eras of Egypt.The cave present here is spread across an area of around 700 meters with 15 meters width and 15 meters depth. The cave was formed as a result of groundwater percolation through the Eocene limestone.Location:Qism Bani Sweif, Bani Sweif, Beni Suef Governorate, EgyptTimings:Open 24 hoursEntry Fee:No entry fee
